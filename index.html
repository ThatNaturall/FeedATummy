Who We Are

Growing up in the streets of Alexandra gave us-the founding team of Feed a tummy- the chance to see
and experience the vast challenges that occur.
One that stood out was seeing the degree of lack of support, hunger and poverty in our township.
Many children go to school hungry demotivated and uncared for.
Therefore, we as feed a tummy (F.A.T) a non-profit organization became dedicated in helping
with the provision of a good and proper meal within the local townships.






Our Initiatives

The Mandela drive was an event celebrating one of
South Africa’s hero and legend Nelson Mandela and
most importantly 67 minutes of doing good.
On this occasion feed a tummy dedicated its time and visited two of Alexandra’s
local nursery schools to provide a meal and snack for the day.
Children received a sandwich, juice, two fruits (orange and apple) and a treat.
This is what the core aim of F.A.T is about which is to feed.
